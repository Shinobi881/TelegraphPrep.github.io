Part 2 Curriculum

Let's put the FUN in functions!

Looking back...

In Part 1 we solidified our JS fundamentals and now have a good understanding of objects, arrays, and functions . We also learnt some good engineering practices like debugging and pseudocoding and started to get into the habit of thinking algorithmically.

Looking forward...

Now we get to explore the power of functions in JavaScript! As well as continue to build more confidence with algorithms.

We'll finish up with a Capstone Project in which you will get to put to use all the awesome JavaScript knowledge you've gained!
